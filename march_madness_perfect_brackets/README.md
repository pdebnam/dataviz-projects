\# March Madness: Perfect Bracket Survival



This project explores how long “perfect” NCAA Tournament brackets survive each year.



\## Why this project?

After my own bracket busted on day one of the 2026 tournament, I wanted to see how

often perfection actually lasts — and whether recent history suggests any trend.



\## Key Takeaways

\- Early upsets play an outsized role

\- Years with #15/#16 seed wins tend to end perfection fast

\- By the first Sunday, most brackets are gone



\## Data

Source: NCAA.com — “The longest an NCAA bracket has ever stayed perfect”

(Updated March 22, 2026)
